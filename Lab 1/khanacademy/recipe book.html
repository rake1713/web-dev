Classic Apple Pie

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Apples 6 large
Cinnamon 1 tsp
Sugar 1 cup

Step 1: Slice the apples and mix with cinnamon and sugar.

Step 2: Put in the oven and bake until golden brown.
